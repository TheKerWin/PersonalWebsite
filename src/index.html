Goals

My one goal is to get better, I thrive on knowing that I can be better tomorrow than I am today and that I have to practice consistently because I can never be the best, there will always be someone better. I find joy in getting better, imagining what is out there that is better than me and imagining what is even beyond that. I constantly feel that I can do so much more(work, progress, contributions to Industry, company, people) than what I am doing now. I have made it this far in my career, why can I not go further? I see every failure as a reason that I need to keep going and I see every success all the more reason not to stop. I am a software engineer but there is no doubt in my mind that one day I can be “The Software Engineer.
